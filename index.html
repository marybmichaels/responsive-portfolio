About Me

Hello, my name is Mary Michaels.

I am originally from Florida, near the Kennedy Space Center & Cocoa Beach. I have a degree in Industrial Design from Auburn University (War Eagle!!!) and I am now working in Austin, Texas as a UX/UI Designer.

Currently, I am learning Full Stack Web Development at the University of Texas at Austin Fullstack Bootcamp. I'm hoping this experience will give me a better understanding of the implications of my designs, as well as aid in collaboration with developers.

I'm excited for this new adventure and hope you enjoy looking through my work!
